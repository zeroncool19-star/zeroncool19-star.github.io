Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Zeroncool ("we", "our", or "the developer") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

Our games collect minimal information to provide and improve the gaming experience:

Information Automatically Collected

  • Game Data: High scores, game progress, and achievements are stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for game optimization.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

Data Storage

All game data (high scores, settings, achievements) is stored locally on your device using your device's local storage. We do not store your personal information on external servers.

Advertisements

Seaweed Swimmer displays ads through Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests. You can manage ad personalization through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete the app and all associated data from your device at any time
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
